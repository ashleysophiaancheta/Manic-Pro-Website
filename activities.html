ATV Riding

Its a form of off-read driving where an ATV (All-Terrain Vehicle) on public trails, a closed course, forest roads, or other off-road riding applications. It can get your cardiovascular workout and strengthen your entire musculoskeletal system.

Hiking

Hiking is a recreational activity that involves walking in nature. Hiking can be done on trails or footpaths in various natural environments, such as mountains, forest, or lakes. This could be done in not so rainy days for safety of our customers.

Horseback Riding

Also known as equestrianism is a sports or activity that involves riding, driving or vaulting a horse. Horse riding can for leisure, recreation, or competition. Horse riding can improve your healthy circulation and its stretches many of your muscles.

Rock Climbing

A climbing wall is a man-made wall with hand and footholds that is typically used for indoor climbing but may also be found outdoors. Made of brick or wood. It will give you an enjoyment to fulfill you bucket list!

Mountain Biking

Mountain bikers ride on off-road trails such as single track, back-country roads, wider bike park trails, fire roads, and some advanced trails are designed with jumps to add excitement to the trail. You can try, practice and make some tricks here!

Skating

The activity of using roller skates to move across surfaces is known as roller skating. It is a sport, a form of recreation, and a mode of transportation. It can provides physical benefits such as improved balance and coordination, stronger bones, and better cardiovascular health.

Camping

It is an outdoor activity that involves staying the night/more than one night in a protective shelter out in nature. Camping is a broad term but in its essence, camping is a way of getting away from the hassle of urban life, to a more natural environment for a limited time. This could be done with your friends, family or trapapits!

Canoeing

It involves a paddling a small craft through water, you can paddle in our own lake for as much you can. This could be done as your hobbies or you can rent this for a competitive sports. It can give you a relaxation and positivity.

Kayaking

It involves in sitting with a small craft with two paddles, it more like with canoeing but this could be done with two paddles while canoeing use a single paddle.

Fishing

It is an activity that you can catch a fish, this will give you an diversion of catching it, or you can practice techniques on how will you catch the fish. After catching you can bring the fish on your own.

Biking

Is an activity that involves a bicycle, you can tour around manic place. There are a lot of bikes that you can use like, mountain bikes, road bicycle, touring bicycle, bicycle for kids and many more!

Hunting

Manic Pro can be your place for practicing hunting. It is the chasing and killing of wild animals for food or as a sport. it is to exploit the animals body for meat and useful animal products. This can give an enjoyment if you like targeting the target.

Photography

It is an activity that 'draws with a light', which derives from the Greek photo, meaning light and graph, meaning to draw. Photography is the process of recording an image a photograph on light sensitive film or, in the case of digital photography, via a digital electronic or magnetic memory.

Picnic

Manic Pro will be a perfect place for picnic. Its an activity that has a social gathering where each attendee brings a share of the food. The French piquer may have referred to a leisurely style of eating ("pick at your food") or it may, simply, have meant, "pick" (pic). This could be done with your family or friends.

Swimming

It is a recreation and sports, the propulsion of the body through water by combined arm and leg motions and the natural flotation of the body. Swimming as an exercise is popular as an all-around body developer and is particularly useful in therapy and as exercise for physically handicapped persons.
